How to Play

1. Click "Start Test" or press SPACEBAR to begin

2. Watch the red lights turn on one by one

3. Wait for the green screen - DON'T click too early!

4. Click or press SPACEBAR as fast as possible when the screen turns green

5. Your reaction time will be recorded

Tips:

• Stay focused on the screen

• Keep your hand ready on the mouse or spacebar

• Practice to improve your times!
